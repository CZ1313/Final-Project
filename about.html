Hi there!

My name is Christina, but I also go by Chris. I am a artist at heart. I have used many different mediums such as charcoal, color pencils, ink and even woodburning! I also dabble in photography, digital art especially using photoshop and make stickers using my cricut. I went to Academy of Art University in San Francisco, CA majoring in visual effects for film. I was lucky enough to work on two films while attending.

However, life had other plans for me, so I wasn't able to pursue a career in that field right after graduation. I went on to learn graphic design at my local community college, College of the Sequoias, CA, while working full time as a office assistant in my hometown. I sometimes refer to myself as a jack-of-all trades!

Currently I am seeing where life may take me next and exploring other potential opportunities. In my spare time I am currently brushing up on my compositing skills and have begun to learn more about coding.

If you would like to reach out, please send me a contact form or click on one of my social media links to learn more about me!
